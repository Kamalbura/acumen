About the Event

Squid Gaming brings the thrill of the popular show to ACUMEN 2025 with a series of challenging childhood games with unique twists. Participants will test their physical abilities, mental agility, strategic thinking, and teamwork in a variety of games inspired by classic playground activities but with added complexity and tech elements.

From Red Light-Green Light with motion sensors to digital shape carving challenges, participants will progress through multiple rounds of increasingly difficult games. Only the most skilled, quick-thinking, and strategic players will survive to claim the ultimate prize. Are you ready to play?

Game Format

Event Structure

The competition consists of 5 games with elimination after each round:

Game 1: Tech Light, Tech Red

A tech-enhanced version of Red Light, Green Light using motion sensors

Game 2: Digital Cookie Cutting

Virtual shape carving challenge requiring precision and steady hands

Game 3: Tug of Code

Teams solve coding challenges to strengthen their position in a virtual tug-of-war

Game 4: Algorithmic Marbles

Strategic game of pattern prediction and algorithm understanding

Final Game: Squid Gateway

Ultimate challenge combining physical activity and technical problem-solving

Rules

  • All participants must follow game-specific rules explained before each round
  • Elimination criteria vary by game
  • Judges' decisions are final
  • Participants must sign a waiver for physical activities
  • Fair play and sportsmanship are expected throughout the competition

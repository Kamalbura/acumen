About the Event

The Poster Presentation competition at ACUMEN 2025 provides an excellent platform for students and researchers to present their work visually and creatively. Through this event, participants can showcase their research findings, innovative ideas, and technical projects in the form of scientific posters.

A successful poster combines the right balance of visual elements with concise text to effectively communicate complex technical concepts to viewers. This event encourages participants to enhance their visual communication skills, design thinking, and ability to present technical information in a succinct, eye-catching format.

Submission Guidelines

Poster Requirements

  • Poster size: A1 format (594 × 841 mm / 23.4 × 33.1 inches)
  • Orientation: Portrait (vertical) format
  • Digital submission: PDF file (for pre-screening) - max 10MB
  • Physical poster to be brought on event day for selected participants
  • Poster content must be original and created specifically for this competition

Content Guidelines

  • Title should be concise and reflect the theme of the poster
  • Author names and institution affiliation should be clearly mentioned
  • Include sections for Introduction, Methodology, Results, Conclusion, and References
  • Use figures, charts, tables, and images to support your content
  • Ensure text is readable from a distance of at least 1 meter

Judging Criteria

Technical Content (35%)

Accuracy, depth, and relevance of the research or project presented

Visual Design (25%)

Layout, color scheme, typography, and overall visual appeal

Clarity & Organization (20%)

Logical flow, ease of understanding, and effective information hierarchy

Presentation Skills (20%)

Ability to explain the poster content and answer questions during evaluation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to print the poster myself?
Yes, participants are responsible for printing and bringing their posters on the day of the event. Make sure your poster adheres to the specified dimensions and is printed on good quality paper.
What topics are suitable for the poster presentation?
We welcome posters on any topic related to computer science and engineering. This includes but is not limited to: artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, IoT, cloud computing, software engineering, and emerging technologies.
How will the poster presentation be conducted?
Posters will be displayed in the exhibition hall throughout the event hours. Each team will be assigned a specific time slot during which they must stand by their poster to present it to the judges and answer questions. Judges will evaluate both the poster itself and your presentation.
Can I use poster templates?
Yes, you can use templates as starting points, but significant customization is expected. Originality in design is one of the judging criteria, so excessive reliance on templates may affect your score in the visual design category.

About the Event

AI Imagination challenges participants to harness the power of artificial intelligence to create innovative solutions for real-world problems. This event invites teams to develop applications, systems, or creative works using various AI technologies including machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, generative AI, and more.

Whether you're designing an AI-powered healthcare assistant, creating generative art, building an intelligent recommendation system, or developing automated solutions for sustainability challenges, this event provides a platform to showcase your technical skills and creative thinking in the rapidly evolving field of AI.

Competition Format

Hackathon Structure

  • Teams will have 24 hours to develop an AI-powered solution from concept to prototype.
  • The event kicks off with domain experts sharing problem statements and opportunities.
  • Teams can choose from suggested challenges or propose their own AI application.
  • Access to various AI platforms and APIs will be provided, including cloud computing resources.
  • Technical mentors will be available throughout the event to assist teams.

Evaluation Criteria

Technical Implementation (30%)

Effective use of AI technologies, code quality, and technical sophistication

Presentation (20%)

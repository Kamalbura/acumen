About the Event

AI Imagination is ACUMEN's premier artificial intelligence hackathon where participants explore the boundaries of creative AI applications. This 24-hour hackathon challenges teams to develop innovative solutions using cutting-edge AI tools including machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and generative models.

Whether you're building an AI-powered healthcare solution, creating generative art, developing intelligent chatbots, or crafting immersive experiences, this event provides a platform to showcase your technical skills and creative thinking. Join us for a full day of innovation, collaboration, and exploration of AI's transformative potential.

Hackathon Format & Themes

Hackathon Structure

  • Teams will have 24 hours to develop an AI solution from concept to functional prototype
  • The event begins with keynote presentations by industry experts highlighting AI challenges
  • Teams can choose from suggested themes or propose their own AI application
  • Technical mentors from leading tech companies will be available throughout the event
  • Final presentations will be 5 minutes plus 3 minutes for Q&A

Theme Areas

Healthcare & Wellbeing

AI solutions for medical diagnosis, mental health, patient monitoring, or personalized healthcare

Generative AI & Creative Applications

AI-generated art, music, content creation tools, or creative assistants

Smart Cities & Sustainability

Solutions for environmental challenges, urban planning, resource optimization, or green tech

Education & Accessibility

Learning tools, accessible technology, personalized education, or knowledge systems

Judging Criteria

Technical Implementation (30%)

Quality of AI technology implementation, code structure, and technical sophistication

Innovation & Creativity (25%)

Uniqueness of approach and creative application of AI technology

Impact & Practicality (25%)

Potential real-world impact, scalability, and practical application

Presentation & Demo (20%)

Quality of demonstration, clarity of explanation, and handling of questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need advanced AI/ML knowledge to participate?
Teams should have at least one member with intermediate programming and AI/ML experience. However, we welcome participants from diverse backgrounds, as creativity and problem-solving are equally important. Mentors will be available to help with technical challenges.
Can we bring pre-built code or projects?
You may use open-source libraries, frameworks, and APIs, but your core implementation must be done during the hackathon. You can come with ideas and plans, but coding should start at the beginning of the event. Pre-built projects are not allowed.
Will we have access to GPU resources?
Yes, we will provide limited GPU access through cloud services for teams working on computationally intensive models. Please specify your resource requirements during registration so we can plan accordingly.
Can we stay overnight at the venue?
Yes, the venue will remain open for the full 24 hours. We'll provide basic sleeping accommodations, but you may want to bring a sleeping bag or pillow for comfort. Shower facilities will also be available.
What happens to our project after the hackathon?
You retain full intellectual property rights to your project. Winners will be featured on the ACUMEN website and social media. Additionally, promising projects may receive mentorship opportunities and introductions to industry partners for further development.

About the Event

The Shark Tank Challenge invites aspiring entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative tech startup ideas to a panel of industry experts and investors. This event provides a platform for students to showcase their business acumen, presentation skills, and creative thinking in a high-stakes environment modeled after the popular TV show.

Participants will develop comprehensive business models, create prototypes or demonstrations, and prepare compelling pitches to convince our panel of "sharks" that their idea is worth investing in. Get ready to defend your concept, answer tough questions, and compete for funding and recognition!

Competition Format

Event Structure

  • Submit your business proposal by March 25, 2025 for pre-screening.
  • Selected teams will be invited to pitch at the event.
  • Each team gets 8 minutes for presentation and 7 minutes for Q&A with the judges.
  • Teams may bring prototypes, demos, or visual aids to support their pitch.
  • Final winners will be announced at the closing ceremony.

Pitch Requirements

  • Clear problem statement and solution description
  • Market analysis and target audience
  • Business model and revenue strategy
  • Technical feasibility and implementation plan
  • Financial projections and funding requirements

Judging Criteria

Innovation & Uniqueness (25%)

Originality of the idea and its potential impact

Business Viability (25%)

Market potential, revenue model, and scalability

Technical Feasibility (20%)

Technical soundness and implementation approach

Presentation & Pitch (20%)

Delivery, clarity, and handling of questions

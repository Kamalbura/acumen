About ACUMEN

ACUMEN is the flagship technical fest of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Vasavi College of Engineering, Hyderabad. It serves as a platform for tech enthusiasts, innovators, and creative minds to showcase their talents, compete, and collaborate.

With a perfect blend of technical and non-technical events, workshops, guest lectures, and hackathons, ACUMEN provides an immersive experience to all participants from across the country.

SCHEDULE

Complete timeline of all events across the three-day extravaganza

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Inauguration Ceremony

Main Auditorium

Official inauguration of ACUMEN 2025 by chief guest Dr. Rajesh Kumar, CTO of CyberTech Solutions.

10:00 AM - 10:00 AM (Next Day)

CyberHack - 24 Hour Hackathon

CSE Department, Main Building

Kickoff for the 24-hour hackathon. Teams will work on their projects with mentorship sessions throughout.

11:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Tech Talk: "Future of AI in 2030"

Seminar Hall

A keynote speech by Dr. Sarah Chen, AI researcher from Google, on the future trends and ethical implications of artificial intelligence.

2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Blockchain Workshop

Lab 201

Hands-on workshop on blockchain technology, smart contracts, and decentralized applications (DApps).

3:00 PM - 6:00 PM

CyberClash: Valorant Tournament (Qualifiers)

Gaming Arena

Preliminary rounds of the Valorant gaming tournament. Top teams will advance to the semi-finals.

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Networking Mixer

Student Activity Center

Connect with industry professionals, alumni, and fellow participants over refreshments. Great opportunity for career discussions.

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Hackathon Presentations (Group 1)

Main Auditorium

First batch of hackathon teams present their solutions to the judges and audience.

11:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Hackathon Presentations (Group 2)

Main Auditorium

Second batch of hackathon teams present their solutions to the judges and audience.

2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Bug Busters - Code Debugging Challenge

Lab 301, CSE Block

Individual coding competition where participants fix bugs in given code snippets.

3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

Panel Discussion: "Cybersecurity in the Digital Age"

Seminar Hall

Industry experts discuss current challenges and future trends in cybersecurity.

5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

CyberClash: FIFA Tournament (Finals)

Gaming Arena

Final matches of the FIFA gaming tournament with live commentary and prizes.

7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Hackathon Results & Cultural Night

Open Air Theater

Announcement of hackathon winners followed by music performances and entertainment.

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Industry Project Exhibition

Exhibition Hall

Showcase of innovative projects by students, startups, and industry partners.

10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

NeuralNexus - AI Challenge

AI Lab, CSE Department

Teams develop and present AI solutions for healthcare data analysis.

11:30 AM - 1:30 PM

Tech Recruiters Meet

Conference Hall

Meet representatives from top tech companies. Bring your resumes for potential internship and job opportunities.

2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

CyberClash: Gaming Finals (All Categories)

Main Auditorium

Grand finals of all gaming tournaments with live streaming and commentary.

4:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Award Ceremony

Main Auditorium

Presentation of awards and certificates to winners of all competitions and events.

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Closing Ceremony & DJ Night

Open Air Theater

Official closing of ACUMEN 2025 followed by music, dance, and celebration.
